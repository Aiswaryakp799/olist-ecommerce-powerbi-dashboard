#  Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

##  Business Problem
Olist, a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace, needed visibility 
into revenue trends, delivery performance, and customer behaviour 
to drive operational and marketing decisions.

##  Business Questions Answered
1. Which product categories and states drive the most revenue?
2. How does delivery performance vary by state and impact 
   customer satisfaction?
3. What are customer payment preferences and retention patterns?

##  Key Insights
- **Health Beauty** is the top revenue category at $1.44M
- Revenue grew consistently from Sep 2016 to Nov 2017 peak of $1.2M/month
- **AL and MA** states have the highest late delivery rates (20%+)
- States with 20+ day delivery average review scores below 3.8/5
- **Credit card** dominates at 75% of all transactions
- Credit card users spend 148% more per order than voucher users
- Only **3% of customers** place repeat orders — major retention opportunity
- **São Paulo** accounts for the highest order volume by far

- 100,000+ real orders from 2016–2018
- 9 relational tables: orders, customers, products, 
  payments, reviews, sellers, geolocation
